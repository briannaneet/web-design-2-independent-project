Flavours

We are proud of offer a variety of unique and fun flavours to delight anyone’s tastebuds. Some flavours change seasonally as we strive to work with local suppliers for most of our ingredients while being inspired by ingredients found in nature- from herbs, to fruits and nuts.

Separate scoops are used for each individual flavour and our nut-containing flavours are processed completely separately from the rest of our offerings to ensure our customer’s safety and enjoyment.
