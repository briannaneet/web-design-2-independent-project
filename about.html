About Us

Merrimint was founded in 2020 when our founder was disappointed with the availability of diary-free ice cream and becoming bored of the same, typical flavours that were offered. It is our mission to provide delicious frozen treats that everyone can enjoy while supporting local suppliers and offering a unique variety of flavours inspired by nature.

All of our flavours are diary-free and vegan, with some options being nut and gluten-free. We use a variety of bases and sweetners
